Discovery Flight

For anyone interested in exploring aviation or just want to experience being a pilot, a Dicovery flight will allow you to pilot a small aircraft! You'll be seated in the cockpit and paired with a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) during your flight. The instructor will spend a few minutes with you explaining what will occur, where you’ll fly, and a game plan for how the flight will go. You’ll also undergo a preflight safety briefing, including how to work the doors and seat belts and where to find the sick sack if you experience air sickness. At the end of your flight you will recieve a flight certificate and become eligible to pursue flight training!

Adventure Flight

For anyone interested in flying for the scenic view rather than piloting, an Adventure Flight is just for you! You and three others can experience a joy flight in a small aircraft and look at the beautiful landscape from high in the skies. Before flight you will recieve a preflight safety briefing, including how to work the doors and seat belts and where to find the sick sack if you experience air sickness.


If you're interested in a Discovery Flight or Adventure Flight, contact us by either going to the Contact Us page or call our phonenumber to schedule!
