About Java Airport

Java Airport is a public general aviation airport located 6908 Skyla Red Clover 158, Schummmouth, Ohio. The airport has one paved 3,004 foot runway, full and connecting taxiways, hangars and tie-downs, maintenance hangar, and a terminal building. Maintenance and repair service, aviation and jet fuel, flight school, and aircraft rentals are available. Picnic tables in front of the terminal building provide a safe and fun area for families to enjoy and watch airplanes taking off and land. Car parking is free.


Java Airpot was founded in 1986. We provide fuel, maintenance and line service to aircraft owners on the field and from elsewhere. We also provide flight instruction and aircraft rentals to local businesses and the general aviation community. We have had an impeccable safety record for all 36 years of operations. All pilots and instructors are FAA certified pilots. Stop in, call, or contact us through our website to schedule your flight or maintenance today!
